About This Site

KCET Cutoffs provides organized cutoff rank data collected from officially published KEA counselling information. The goal of this website is to help students quickly search and analyze cutoff trends from previous years and upcoming counselling rounds.

Features include:

  • Previous year KCET cutoff ranks
  • College and branch-wise filtering
  • Category-wise cutoff comparison
  • Easy mobile-friendly access
  • Fast search and navigation

This platform is independently created to simplify KCET counselling research for students and parents.
